I was a visiting scholar in the School of Electrical, Computer and Energy Engineering at Arizona State University, Tempe, USA, in 2016. Currently, I am a full-time Professor at the Aeronautics Institute of Technology in São José dos Campos, São Paulo, Brazil. My research interests are data science, machine learning, complex networks, and complex systems.
